Experiments
  • Free vibration of cantilever beam
  • Free vibration of simply supported beam
  • Free vibration of fixed beam
  • Forced vibration of SDOF system
  • Base Excitation
  • Rotating Unbalance
  • 2DOF Forced vibration
  • Dynamic Vibration Absorber


Target Audience

This course is primarily for pre-final and/or final year students of Bachelor's course in Mechanical Engineering.

This course is also useful for anyone who is working in the field of Acoustics.

Courses Aligned

Following are the courses in which one can come across the theory connected to the experiments in this lab

  • Strength of Materials
  • Dynamics of Machines
  • Mechanical Vibrations

You can find a list of other related labs here

Pre-Requisites

Pre-requisites

This lab is related to a basic course on Dynamics and Vibrations. There are no mandatory pre-requisites however a good understanding of Engineering Mechanics and Differential Equations is necessary.

Software Requirement

Adobe Flash Plugin is needed to run some of the simulation experiments on the browser. You can download it for free here. Kindly wait for flash file to load when opening a simulation. Depending on your internet connection speed and the number of users accessing our site it may take a minute.

For Javascript based simulations you do not need Adobe Flash however Javascript must be enabled in your browser. Please use latest Mozilla Firefox or Google Chrome browser for best results
